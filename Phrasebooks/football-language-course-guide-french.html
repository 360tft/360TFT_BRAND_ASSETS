Cultural Notes:

Formal vs Informal: Use "vous" (formal you) with parents, officials, and players you don't know well. Use "tu" (informal) with players once rapport is established.

Greetings: The "bise" (cheek kiss) is common in France, typically 2 kisses but varies by region. A handshake is always safe.

Francophone Africa: French is widely spoken in Senegal, Ivory Coast, Cameroon, Morocco, Algeria. Respect for elders and hierarchy is important.
