Cultural Notes:

Portugal vs Brazil: Vocabulary differs significantly. "Goleiro" (Brazil) vs "Guarda-redes" (Portugal) for goalkeeper. Brazilian Portuguese is more commonly understood globally.

Communication style: Portuguese/Brazilian culture values relationship-building. Take time for greetings and small talk before getting to business.

Physical contact: Expect handshakes, pats on the back, and closer personal space than Anglo cultures.
