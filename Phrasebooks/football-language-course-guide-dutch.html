Cultural Notes:

Direct communication: Dutch people are famously direct. What may seem blunt is considered honest and efficient.

Total Football philosophy: The Netherlands is home to Total Football - expect players to understand positional fluidity and pressing concepts.

Informal address: Use "je/jij" (informal you) with players and even parents in sports settings. "U" (formal) sounds overly stiff.

Punctuality: Like Germans, Dutch people value being on time.
