## Common Mistakes & False Friends

### Words That Don't Mean What You Think

| English Word | Spanish Trap | Actual Meaning | Correct Spanish |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Assist (in football) | Asistir | To attend | Asistencia |
| Embarrassed | Embarazada | Pregnant! | Avergonzado |
| Excited | Excitado | Sexually aroused! | Emocionado |
| Sensible | Sensible | Sensitive | Sensato |
| Actually | Actualmente | Currently | En realidad |
| Support | Soportar | To tolerate | Apoyar |
| Realize | Realizar | To accomplish | Darse cuenta |

### Cultural Notes

| Situation | UK/US Approach | Spanish/Latin American Approach |
|-----------|----------------|--------------------------------|
| Criticism | Direct, immediate | Often softened, relationship first |
| Time | Punctual expected | More flexibility (varies by country) |
| Physical contact | Handshake | Handshake, often with shoulder touch |
| Volume | Moderate | Generally louder, more animated |
| Eye contact | Important but not intense | Very important, shows respect |
| Hierarchy | First name basis common | May use titles with directors/presidents |
