Cultural Notes:

Gesture is language: Italians communicate heavily with hand gestures. Learn the common ones - they add meaning to what you're saying.

Respect for the coach: Italian football culture has deep respect for the "Mister" (coach). Your role carries authority.

Tactical culture: Italy is known for tactical sophistication. Players expect detailed tactical instructions.

Emotion: Italian football is emotional. Passionate feedback is expected and appreciated.
