Cultural Notes:

Respect is paramount: Always greet with "As-salamu alaykum" (Peace be upon you). The response is "Wa alaykum as-salam".

Ramadan: During Ramadan, training times shift and players fast from dawn to sunset. Be mindful of intensity and hydration around Iftar time.

Prayer times: Players may need breaks for prayer (5 times daily). Build flexibility into your schedule.

Physical contact: Handshakes are common between men. Be culturally sensitive about interactions.

Hierarchy: Age and seniority are respected. Address seniors and officials formally.
